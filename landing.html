Safety First

A safe space to
make mistakes

Learning a language means getting things wrong sometimes. That's why lingr is built to be a judgment-free, supportive community where everyone is here to grow.

Zero tolerance policy

Any inappropriate language, harassment, or disrespectful behavior results in an immediate and permanent ban. No exceptions, no second chances.

Judgment-free zone

Wrong conjugation? Funny pronunciation? That's the whole point of being here. Every member commits to being patient, kind, and encouraging.

Constructive feedback only

When correcting someone, be kind about it. Explain the "why" and celebrate progress. We learn faster when we feel safe making mistakes.

Easy reporting system

See something wrong? One tap to report it. Our team reviews every report within 24 hours and takes swift action to protect the community.

Your safety is our priority

All meetup locations are vetted, all profiles are verified through IE University email, and our community guidelines are enforced 24/7. Learn languages without worry.
